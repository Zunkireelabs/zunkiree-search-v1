Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: May 28, 2026
Last Updated: May 28, 2026

This page explains what data Zunkiree Labs holds about you and how to request its deletion. For full details on how we collect and use data, see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Data We Hold

1.1 End-User Data (Automated Messaging)

When you send a direct message to a business that uses the Zunkiree Search Automated Messaging service (via Instagram, Facebook Messenger, or WhatsApp), we may store:

  • Message content — The text of messages you send and receive.
  • Platform sender ID — A platform-specific identifier assigned by Meta (not your username or full profile).
  • Conversation history — Prior messages in the thread, retained temporarily to provide contextual responses.
  • Message log entries — Audit records used for deduplication and debugging.

1.2 Client Business Account Data

If you are a business using the Zunkiree Search platform, we hold your account credentials, knowledge base content, channel configuration, and analytics data associated with your subscription. Contact your account manager or email us for business account deletion.

2. How to Request Deletion

2.1 Primary Method — Email Request

Send an email to info@zunkireelabs.com with:

  • Subject line: Data Deletion Request
  • The platform where you interacted (Instagram, Facebook Messenger, or WhatsApp).
  • Your platform username or handle, or the approximate timeframe of your interactions if you prefer not to share your username.

We will use this information to locate and delete your stored data as described in Section 3.

2.2 Immediate Stop via Platform Block

If you want to stop further data collection immediately, you can block the business's account on the Meta platform (Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp). This prevents any new messages from being processed by our system. Note: blocking does not retroactively delete data already stored — use the email process above to request that.

2.3 For Client Businesses

To request deletion of your business account and all associated data, contact your account manager directly or email info@zunkireelabs.com with your merchant slug and a description of the data you want removed.

3. What Happens After Your Request

Timeline

  • Acknowledgement — Within 3 business days of receiving your request.
  • Deletion completed — Within 30 days of acknowledgement.

What Gets Deleted

  • All stored messages and conversation history tied to your platform sender ID.
  • Your cached sender profile (display name, username).
  • Query and message log entries linked to your sender ID.

What Cannot Be Deleted

  • Aggregate analytics — Anonymized, aggregated statistics that do not identify you individually cannot be removed, as they contain no personal information.
  • Meta platform data — Data that Meta retains on their own platform (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp) is outside our control. Deletion of that data is handled per Meta's own data deletion process.

4. Automatic Deletion

Most users do not need to file a deletion request. Our platform automatically deletes:

  • Conversation history — After 7 days (per our Privacy Policy §4).
  • Message logs — After 90 days.

If you want your data removed sooner than these automatic timelines, use the email process in Section 2.
