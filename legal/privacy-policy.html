Zunkiree Search

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026
Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Zunkiree Labs ("we", "us", "our") operates the Zunkiree Search platform, which provides AI-powered search widgets and automated messaging services for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with our services.

1. Services Covered

This policy covers the following Zunkiree Search services:

  • Search Widget — An AI-powered search tool embedded on our clients' websites that answers visitor questions using the client's own content.
  • Automated Messaging — AI-powered automated replies on social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp) on behalf of our clients.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Search Widget

When you use the search widget on a client's website, we may collect:

  • Search queries — The questions you type into the widget.
  • Email address and name — Only if voluntarily provided during identity verification for personalized responses.
  • Session data — A randomly generated session identifier (not linked to your personal identity).

2.2 Automated Messaging (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp)

When you send a direct message to one of our clients' social media accounts that uses our automated messaging service, we receive:

  • Your message content — The text of your message.
  • Platform sender ID — A platform-specific identifier assigned by Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp (not your username or profile data).
  • Conversation history — Previous messages in the conversation thread, retained temporarily to provide contextual responses.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your social media profile, photos, followers, or any account data beyond what is described above.
  • We do not collect payment information, location data, or device identifiers.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Search queries To generate relevant AI-powered answers from the client's knowledge base
Messages To generate automated responses using the client's knowledge base
Conversation history To provide contextual follow-up responses within a conversation
Email / name To personalize responses when voluntarily provided
Query logs To help our clients understand what their visitors are asking about

4. Data Retention

  • Conversation history (messaging) — Automatically deleted after 7 days.
  • Message logs (messaging) — Retained for up to 90 days for audit and debugging purposes, then deleted.
  • Search query logs — Retained for the duration of the client's subscription to provide analytics.
  • User profiles (email, name) — Retained for the duration of the client's subscription and deleted upon request.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

Service Purpose Data Shared
OpenAI AI language model for generating answers Search queries and message content (processed, not stored by OpenAI per their API data usage policy)
Pinecone Vector database for semantic search Numerical embeddings of client content (not user data)
Meta Platform (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp) Receiving and sending messages Message content and platform sender IDs as required by Meta's API

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit — All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encryption at rest — Sensitive credentials (such as platform access tokens) are encrypted using industry-standard encryption (Fernet/AES).
  • Multi-tenant isolation — Each client's data is strictly isolated. Your messages to one business are never accessible to another business.
  • Webhook verification — All incoming messages are verified using HMAC-SHA256 signatures to prevent tampering.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Opt out — Stop interacting with the widget or messaging service at any time. Conversation history will be automatically deleted after 7 days.
  • Correction — Request correction of any inaccurate data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
